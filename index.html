What We Treat

Expert Physiotherapy Services

From chronic pain to neurological rehab — our evidence-based treatments are tailored to your unique needs.

🦴

Orthopaedic Physiotherapy

Comprehensive care for musculoskeletal conditions including joint pain, fracture rehab, sports injuries, and post-surgical recovery.

🧠

Neuro Physiotherapy

Specialized rehabilitation for stroke, Parkinson's, multiple sclerosis, and other neurological disorders to restore movement and independence.

🔙

Spine & PIVD Rehab

Expert management of Prolapsed Intervertebral Disc, sciatica, spondylosis, and all spinal conditions with targeted decompression therapy.

👶

Paediatric Physiotherapy

Gentle, play-based therapy for children with developmental delays, cerebral palsy, torticollis, and movement disorders.

👴

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Helping seniors regain balance, strength, and mobility. Fall prevention, arthritis management, and age-related condition care.

🧘

Postural Training

Corrective programs for forward head posture, rounded shoulders, and workplace-related postural dysfunctions for long-term relief.
